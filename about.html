About EverAfter Planners

Designing memorable events with passion and 2 years of dedication.

Our Story

EverAfter Planners began with a dream to create events that go beyond celebrations moments that leave a lasting impact. Since our humble start in 2018, we’ve grown from a small initiative to a trusted name in event management, recognized for creativity, precision, and heartfelt service.

Our journey has been driven by a deep passion for designing unforgettable experiences. From weddings and milestone birthdays to corporate galas and cultural events, we’ve had the privilege of turning countless visions into reality.

With a talented team of planners, designers, and coordinators, we focus on every detail from concept creation and theme planning to flawless execution so you can enjoy your special moments without stress.

At EverAfter Planners, every event tells a unique story, and we are honored to help you tell yours with elegance, creativity, and love.
